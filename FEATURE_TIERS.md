# Compliance Instance - SaaS Feature Tiers

## 🎯 Tiering Strategy Overview

This document outlines the three-tier pricing model for the Compliance Management SaaS platform:
- **Lite** - Essential compliance for small teams and startups
- **Pro** - Advanced compliance for growing organizations
- **Enterprise** - Complete compliance suite for large enterprises

## 🎯 **Recommended Tier by Use Case**

### Choose **LITE** if you:
- Are a startup or small business (< 10 employees)
- Need **basic compliance management** without advanced modules
- Want to **track requirements, controls, and evidence** manually
- Don't need risk management, audit modules, or advanced analytics
- Want to get started quickly with minimal investment
- Don't need automation, integrations, or API access
- **Example:** Early-stage SaaS startup preparing for first SOC 2 audit

### Choose **PRO** if you:
- Are a growing company (10-100 employees)
- Need **advanced modules**: Risk Management, Internal Audit, Executive Dashboards
- Want **workflow automation** and multi-role approval processes
- Need **advanced compliance scoring** and analytics
- Require **integrations** with Slack, Teams, calendars
- Have dedicated compliance/security team
- Want **playbooks and guided workflows** for implementation
- **Example:** Series B company managing SOC 2, ISO 27001, and GDPR simultaneously

### Choose **ENTERPRISE** if you:
- Are a large organization (100+ employees)
- Operate in **highly regulated industries** (finance, healthcare, government)
- Need **AI-powered modules**: Regulatory Intelligence, Predictive Compliance
- Want **visual builder** to create custom frameworks and workflows
- Require **continuous monitoring** and fully automated controls
- Need **multi-tenant** support for subsidiaries or business units
- Want **SSO/SAML**, custom branding, and enterprise integrations
- Require **24/7 support**, dedicated CSM, and guaranteed SLAs
- Need to **track regulatory changes** and manage multiple authorities
- **Example:** Financial institution managing 20+ compliance frameworks across multiple jurisdictions
