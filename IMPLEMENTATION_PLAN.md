# Comprehensive CBUAE Regulatory Implementation Plan

## Objective
Implement realistic, production-grade CBUAE regulatory framework with proper scale:
- **100+ Programs** across all CBUAE regulatory modules
- **1000+ Requirements** (50+ per major program)
- **2000+ Controls** (200+ per major program)
- **500+ Obligations** with proper timelines

## CBUAE Major Regulatory Modules (Based on Research)

### 1. **Credit Risk Management Regulation**
- Requirements: 60+
- Controls: 250+
- Obligations: 25+
- Coverage: Credit policies, concentration risk, provisioning, stress testing

### 2. **Liquidity Risk Management & Supervision**
- Requirements: 55+
- Controls: 220+
- Obligations: 20+
- Coverage: LCR, NSFR, liquidity buffers, stress testing, contingency funding

### 3. **Capital Adequacy Standards (Basel III)**
- Requirements: 70+
- Controls: 280+
- Obligations: 30+
- Coverage: CET1, Tier 1, Tier 2, risk-weighted assets, capital buffers

### 4. **AML/CFT Guidelines for Financial Institutions**
- Requirements: 80+
- Controls: 300+
- Obligations: 35+
- Coverage: CDD, EDD, transaction monitoring, STR/SAR, sanctions screening

### 5. **Consumer Protection Standards**
- Requirements: 50+
- Controls: 200+
- Obligations: 15+
- Coverage: Fair treatment, transparency, complaints handling, product suitability

### 6. **Operational Risk Management**
- Requirements: 65+
- Controls: 260+
- Obligations: 22+
- Coverage: Business continuity, IT systems, outsourcing, fraud prevention

### 7. **Market Risk Management**
- Requirements: 45+
- Controls: 180+
- Obligations: 18+
- Coverage: Trading book, VaR, stress testing, position limits

### 8. **Interest Rate Risk in Banking Book (IRRBB)**
- Requirements: 40+
- Controls: 160+
- Obligations: 12+
- Coverage: Gap analysis, duration, EVE, NII sensitivity

### 9. **Country and Transfer Risk Standards**
- Requirements: 35+
- Controls: 140+
- Obligations: 10+
- Coverage: Cross-border exposures, country risk assessment, provisioning

### 10. **Corporate Governance Standards**
- Requirements: 55+

- Obligations: 20+
- Coverage: Board composition, risk committees, internal audit, compliance function

## Implementation Approach

1. **Create separate data files** for each major module
2. **Generate realistic requirements** based on actual regulatory content
3. **Map controls** to requirements with proper traceability
4. **Define obligations** with realistic timelines and frequencies
5. **Link evidence** requirements to each control
6. **Assign owners** across different departments
