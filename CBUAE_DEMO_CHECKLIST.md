## ❓ FAQ - Be Prepared to Answer

### Q1: "How did you map all these CBUAE requirements?"
**A**: "We have a team of compliance experts who have analyzed all CBUAE regulations and circulars. Each requirement is mapped to specific regulation articles, and we continuously update as CBUAE issues new guidance."

### Q2: "Can we customize the requirements?"
**A**: "Absolutely. While we provide pre-mapped requirements as a starting point, you can add, modify, or remove requirements to match your specific needs and risk profile."

### Q3: "How often do you update the frameworks?"
**A**: "We monitor CBUAE regulatory updates continuously. When CBUAE issues new regulations or updates existing ones, we update the frameworks within 30 days and notify all users."

### Q4: "Can we import multiple frameworks at once?"
**A**: "Yes, you can import as many frameworks as you need. Many of our UAE customers import all core banking frameworks (IT Gov, Cyber, AML/CFT, Consumer Protection, Outsourcing) to get comprehensive coverage."

### Q5: "How does this integrate with our existing systems?"
**A**: "Our platform can integrate with your existing GRC tools, document management systems, and ticketing systems via APIs. We also support data import/export in standard formats."

### Q6: "What about evidence management?"
**A**: "Each control and test has defined evidence requirements. You can upload evidence documents, link them to controls and tests, and track approval workflows. Everything is version-controlled and audit-ready."

### Q7: "Can we track obligations and deadlines?"
**A**: "Yes, all CBUAE reporting obligations are pre-loaded with frequencies and typical due dates. The system sends reminders before deadlines and tracks submission status."

### Q8: "How do you handle Arabic language requirements?"
**A**: "While the platform interface is in English, we support Arabic content for requirements, controls, and evidence. We can also provide Arabic translations of CBUAE regulations upon request."

### Q9: "What about CBUAE Rulebook updates?"
**A**: "We track the CBUAE Rulebook continuously. When new regulations are published or existing ones are updated, we update our frameworks and notify affected users with change summaries."

### Q10: "Can we see a full implementation timeline?"
**A**: "Typically, customers can import frameworks and start tracking compliance within 1 week. Full implementation with all controls tested and evidence uploaded takes 8-12 weeks depending on organization size."
