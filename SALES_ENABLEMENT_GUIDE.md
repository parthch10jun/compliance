# Sales Enablement Guide - Compliance Instance SaaS

## Quick Reference Card

### Tier Qualification Questions

**Ask these 5 questions to qualify the right tier:**

1. **"How many people will be using the platform?"**
   - 1-3 people → Lite
   - 4-10 people → Pro
   - 10+ people → Pro or Enterprise

2. **"How many compliance frameworks are you managing?"**
   - 1 framework, basic tracking → Lite
   - Multiple frameworks, need obligations tracking → Pro
   - 5+ frameworks, complex requirements → Enterprise

3. **"Do you need to track time-bound obligations with deadlines?"**
   - No → Lite is fine
   - Yes → Pro minimum (Obligations module)

4. **"Do you need risk management or internal audit capabilities?"**
   - No → Lite is fine
   - Yes → Pro minimum

5. **"Are you in a highly regulated industry (finance, healthcare, government)?"**
   - No → Lite or Pro
   - Yes, with regulatory change tracking needs → Enterprise

Unlike competitors, we don't limit your compliance data - you get unlimited requirements, controls, and evidence. Competitors charge $500-$1,000/month and limit you to 50 requirements. What's your budget?"

> "Let me show you what's included in Pro that competitors charge Enterprise prices for: Risk Management, Internal Audit, Obligations tracking, and Executive Dashboards. Vanta charges $2,000+/month for these features. At $999/month, you're getting Enterprise features at Pro pricing. What features are most important to you?"
