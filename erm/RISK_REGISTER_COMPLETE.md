## 🚀 **How to Use**

### **Navigate:**
1. Go to http://localhost:3006/erm
2. Click "View Risk Register" button
3. OR click sidebar → "Risk Management" → Navigate to `/erm/risk-register`

### **Search:**
- Type in search box to filter by ID, title, or owner
- Real-time filtering as you type

### **Filter:**
- Select category from dropdown (All, Cybersecurity, Operational, etc.)
- Select status from dropdown (All, Identified, Treated, etc.)

### **Sort:**
- Click any column header to sort
- Click again to reverse sort direction
- Arrow shows current sort (▲ asc, ▼ desc)

### **View Details:**
- Click any row to view risk details (modal coming soon)
- Click eye icon for quick view
