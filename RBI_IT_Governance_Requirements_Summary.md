# RBI IT Governance Master Direction - Complete Requirements Mapping

## Program Overview

**Regulator:** Reserve Bank of India (RBI)  
**Framework:** Master Direction - IT Governance, Risk, Controls & Assurance Practices  
**Reference:** RBI/DOR/2023-24/81  
**Issued Date:** November 7, 2023  
**Effective Date:** April 1, 2024  
**Status:** Active

---

## Applicability Matrix

| Layer | Asset Size Criteria | Compliance Level | Applicable |
|-------|-------------------|------------------|------------|
| **Top Layer** | ≥ ₹50,000 crore | Full Compliance | ✅ Yes |
| **Upper Layer** | ≥ ₹1,000 crore and < ₹50,000 crore | Substantial Compliance | ✅ Yes |
| **Middle Layer** | ≥ ₹500 crore and < ₹1,000 crore | Moderate Compliance | ✅ Yes |
| **Base Layer** | < ₹500 crore | Not Applicable | ❌ No |
