## 🔗 Linkages & Relationships

### Requirements → Obligations
Each requirement is linked to relevant obligations:
- IT Strategy (REQ-001) → Annual Report (OBL-001)
- Risk Assessment (REQ-005) → Annual Risk Assessment (OBL-003)
- Business Continuity (REQ-011) → BCP Test (OBL-004)
- Disaster Recovery (REQ-012) → DR Test (OBL-005)
- Incident Response (REQ-015) → Incident Reporting (OBL-006)

### Requirements → Controls
Each requirement is linked to implementing controls:
- IT Strategy (REQ-001) → IT Strategy Document (CTRL-001), IT Steering Committee (CTRL-002)
- Access Control (REQ-007) → MFA (CTRL-007), Access Review (CTRL-008)
- Encryption (REQ-008) → Encryption at Rest (CTRL-009), Encryption in Transit (CTRL-010)
- Cyber Security (REQ-014) → Firewall (CTRL-017), IDS/IPS (CTRL-018), Anti-Malware (CTRL-019)

### Controls → Obligations
Controls are linked to obligations they help satisfy:
- BCP (CTRL-014) → BCP Test Obligation (OBL-004)
- DR (CTRL-015) → DR Test Obligation (OBL-005)
- Incident Response (CTRL-021) → Incident Reporting (OBL-006)
